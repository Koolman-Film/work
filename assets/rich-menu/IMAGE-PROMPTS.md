# Rich-menu button art — complete image-generation prompts

Each prompt below is **self-contained** (full style + exact colors + exact text) —
copy one block straight into your image generator.

## ⚠️ Read first: the text caveat

Image generators render **Latin** text well but frequently **garble Thai, Khmer,
Lao, Myanmar, and Chinese** glyphs. Two ways to use these prompts:

- **Mode A — full tile (text included, as written below).** Fastest, but inspect
  every non-Latin glyph; regenerate until the Thai headline and the 5-language
  subtitle are correct. Expect several tries.
- **Mode B — art only (safest).** Delete the two "TEXT:" lines from a prompt, keep
  only background + illustration, then composite the headline + subtitles as vector
  over the art (the `.svg` masters in this folder already do this with pixel-perfect
  fonts). Recommended for production — guaranteed-correct multilingual text.

**Format:** square tiles = 1:1; the wide check-in banner = 3:1 landscape.
Generate at the highest resolution available, then upscale to the menu size
(2500-px wide).

## Negative prompt (use with any of the above)

> extra text, misspelled letters, gibberish glyphs, watermark, signature, logo,
> people, hands, busy background, harsh shadows, photorealistic clutter, neon.

## Layout reference (so the art lands in the right zone)

| Tile | Art zone | Text zone |
|---|---|---|
| Wide check-in (3:1) | right third | left two-thirds |
| Square tiles (1:1) | top ~40% | bottom ~60% |

Keep each illustration's dominant color equal to that tile's accent hex so art and
panel feel unified. Brand palette: blue #2D6CDF · orange #E08A2B · green #2EA86A ·
purple #7C5BD6, on pastels #E8F1FC / #FBF1E8 / #E9F6EE / #F1ECFB.
